About



Accompanied by Tun-A-Toon - her loyal dog, Mel - the angel starts her journey to the 33 wonders of Indonesia.

Angels Project NFTs are 1366 unique NFTS from 3 different collections that symbolize three different element in the world.

1,111 Tun-a-toon, a charming dog with unique traits and emotions that depicts the animal in the first phase

222 Mel the angel or devil or something in between who represents human nature in the second phase

222 Mel the angel or devil or something in between who represents human nature in the second phase

33 Wonders - hand drawing floating islands of Indonesian archipelago representing the world in the final phase

These three interconnected collections are classed by rarity and stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Polygon blockchain.

Angels Project was initiated by Rumah Harapan and Melanie Subono – an Indonesian famous artist that also known as activist that has made many social movements to help others in need. This formed Angel’s project not just as collectible art but also “social movement" as an identity.

By owning Angels Project NFTs, you can participate to help people, nature and animals in need through Rumah Harapan.

Rumah Harapan

is a 14-year-old movement started by Melanie Subono and her team with the goal of assisting local communities in need. Rumah Harapan has aided a wide range of people in need, including children, the elderly, and also animals in Indonesia.
